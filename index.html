About Me:

My name is Joshua Hunsche Jones and I am a software developer. I began my career studying cinema and media communications at George Fox University. Following my graduation, I worked in a project coordinator role at FedEx office then moved into the health care industry with several roles at Providence Health plan.

In 2017, I stumbled onto the field of software development while working on a training project and I was hooked. The balance of the intense intellectual challenge, creative engagement, and detailed problem solving fit me perfectly. I immersed myself in coding books and enrolled at The Tech Academy, a software development boot camp in Portland, Oregon.

With my experience building projects in HTML, CSS, C#, ASP.NET, Node.js and Express.js--as well as the team projects I have completed during my internship at Providence Health Plan, I have gained valuable skills that I am looking forward to using in a full time developer role in the Portland area.

Example website built in Bootstrap for mobile or desktop

The biggest hurdle I overcame on this project was the nav bar. Once I fixed it to the top of the window, my div id navigation resulted in some of the page content becoming obscured. I tried a couple solutions and settled on using separate anchor tags so I could target them using negative positioning values in my CSS (without messing with the way bootstrap’s CSS was applying.) The final site looks great on all kinds of screens and it was really fun to build in bootstrap’s framework.
